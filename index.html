The end of an era.

So I shifted to clerical work and later, software development using the Microsoft Office suite. For the next twenty years or so I had a career in Tech Support. That ended in 2018. Crap. So . . . back to driving people for dollars. I did that until this spring (April 2023). Now that's done. Baugh Holding Company will continue. Doing what? We shall see.
